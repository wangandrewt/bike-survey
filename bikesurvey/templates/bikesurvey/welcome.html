How to count: Be certain you are tallying bikes during your assigned time block. As you observe bicyclists passing your post, note each one in the following form. (Do not count motorized scooters, motorcycles, mopeds, or razor-style scooters, or bicycles being walked by a pedestrian.)

  • Location: Riding in the street or the sidewalk; crossing street in a crosswalk should be counted as sidewalk.
