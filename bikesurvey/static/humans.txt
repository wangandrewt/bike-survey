# humanstxt.org/

A web application to record information about bikes on campus.

Created by Andrew T Wang (wtandrew@terpmail.umd.edu), a student at the
 University of Maryland, College Park for the Department of Transportation
 Services and the Department of Sustainability

Uses code from
 OpenShift (https://github.com/openshift/django-example) and
 Appsembler (https://github.com/appsembler/paasbakeoff/tree/openshift)
